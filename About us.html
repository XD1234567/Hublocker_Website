Who we are

Welcome to HubLocker Storage, your premier destination
for secure and convenient storage solutions.

With 5 years of experience in the industry, we understand the importance of providing top-notch storage services tailored to our customers' needs. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced security systems, ensuring that your belongings are protected around the clock. At HubLocker Storage, we take pride in our clean and well-maintained lockers, available in a variety of sizes to accommodate any storage requirement. Convenience is at the heart of our service, with extended access hours that allow you to access your items whenever it suits your schedule. With our easy-to-use online portal, managing your storage unit is a breeze, allowing you to make payments and reservations effortlessly. We value the safety of your belongings as much as you do, which is why we have implemented stringent security measures to ensure peace of mind. Choose HubLocker Storage for reliable, secure, and convenient storage solutions. Let us help you declutter and create more space in your life.

Our Vision

At Oceangrsmith our vision is to create a better way for many people in Africa to receive online and offline purchase order through a secured digital Hublocker with a paired application. Our business idea supports this vision by offering a service that will allow customers at a place and time that’s convenient for them.

Our Mission Statement

To implement a computerized parcel locker delivery in Nigeria. The computerized parcel lockers will be sited at different designated location across the country. This solution will enhance the delivery of packages and pick-up in NIgeria.
